MAKE A DONATION

Still today, our organization is managed by passionate volunteers. Although we are looking for grants that would provide operating and management salaries to members of the organizational core, we are constantly faced with a lack of investment. At this time where darkrooms are victims of gentrification too, your support is precious!

Help us ensure the longevity of our collective darkroom. Donate to infotrounoir@gmail.com! We also accept donations of darkroom equipment (film cameras, photographic paper, film, etc.) with great pleasure! Do not hesitate to contact us about this.
